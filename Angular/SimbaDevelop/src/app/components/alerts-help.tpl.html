845-2243

Emergency Notifications Self-Registration Portal

The Emergency Notifications Self Registration Portal (SRP) is where you can sign up for alerts (protective actions, gate status, work delays/closures) to improve notifications during emergencies and routine information to assist with business decision making. The SRP is a highly secure yet easy–to–access web tool that allows people to sign up to receive the notifications issued by Emergency Management. The SRP has been reviewed and approved by Cyber Security and Legal.
