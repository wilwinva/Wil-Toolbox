MBE Standards and Resources

MBE is moving toward a standardization needed for a complex-wide adoption. As a major step in this direction, the Department of Defense (DoD) released the MIL-STD-31000A standard, which defines a technical data package (TDP) and outlines modifications to support an MBE lifecycle transformation, providing guidance on the following:

  • 2D and 3D TDP comparisons
  • Alignment between the TDP and the product lifecycle to enable acquisition of the right data at the right point in the lifecycle
  • 3D TDP definition based on modern data to provide a product definition foundation that can be reused throughout the lifecycle
  • Complete up-to-date TDP definition that can be used to competitively bid a product
  • Method for structuring and verifying the quality of a 3D TDP

DoD is also working to transition this standard to NIST.
