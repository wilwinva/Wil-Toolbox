  3. Can I use Flextime?

Answer

Exempt employees typically receive no extra pay for hours worked beyond their defined work schedule. However, with prior management approval , flextime may be accrued when business needs create situations that justifies it use to meet business needs. Flextime is earned for the two hours plus any additional hours beyond the two for time worked. Take flextime off in one-hour increments using TRC 288. Flextime may not be taken before it is accumulated. Flextime is not an "automatic" benefit. A maximum rolling balance of 80 hours will apply to flextime during the fiscal year. Additional flextime will not be accrued until the employeeâ€™s balance is below 80 hours. Flextime not used prior to termination of employment is forfeited.
